# Persona Validation Survey — Club Padel Players

**Purpose.** The professor's evaluation flagged that "personas are drawn even if it is not clear what was their foundation." This survey is the foundation: it is administered to real club players *before* (or independently of) any app testing, purely to check whether the traits assumed for "Marco, the club player" and "Luca, the amateur coach" (see `01_Brief/01_brief.md`, §1.3) actually hold in a small local sample.

**Who administers it.** The student, to 3–5 club padel players (any club, any level — the brief targets *amateur* players, not competitive ones) and, if available, 1 amateur coach for the Luca-specific items.

**Format.** Paper or Google Forms, ~5 minutes to complete. Anonymize responses as `P1, P2, P3…` — never store names against answers. Keep raw responses in `07_Validation/results/` as `P1_persona_survey.md` (or `.pdf`/exported `.csv`), one file per participant.

**Consent line to read/show before starting (Italian respondents):**

> EN: *"This short survey supports a university data-visualization project. Answers are anonymized (you'll be referred to as P1, P2…) and used only for this coursework. It takes about 5 minutes. You can skip any question."*
> IT: *"Questo breve questionario supporta un progetto universitario di data visualization. Le risposte sono anonime (sarai indicato/a come P1, P2…) e usate solo per questo lavoro di corso. Richiede circa 5 minuti. Puoi saltare qualsiasi domanda."*
