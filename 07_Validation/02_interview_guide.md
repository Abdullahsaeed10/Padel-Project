# Semi-Structured Interview Guide (15 minutes)

**Purpose.** A short, spoken follow-up to the persona survey (`01_persona_survey.md`) that digs into *why* behind the survey's *what*, and produces direct quotes usable on the personas slide. Run this with 2–3 of the same club players (or classmates acting as proxies if club players are scarce), ideally before they see the app, so answers aren't biased by what they just used.

**Format.** 1-on-1, ~15 minutes, audio-recorded with verbal consent (or notes only, if the participant declines recording). Anonymize as P1, P2… Save notes to `07_Validation/results/P#_interview_notes.md`.

**Consent script (read aloud):**
> EN: *"Thanks for taking 15 minutes. I'll ask about how you currently think about your own padel game — there are no right or wrong answers. If it's okay, I'll [record this / take notes] and quote you anonymously as P[n] in my project. You can stop at any time."*
> IT: *"Grazie per questi 15 minuti. Ti farò qualche domanda su come pensi al tuo gioco a padel — non ci sono risposte giuste o sbagliate. Se per te va bene, [registrerò / prenderò appunti] e ti citerò in forma anonima come P[n] nel mio progetto. Puoi interrompere in qualsiasi momento."*

---

## Warm-up (not scored, ~1 min)
- "How long have you been playing, and how often?" / *"Da quanto tempo giochi, e quanto spesso?"*

## Core Questions (5) + Probes

### Q1 — The felt-vs-confirmed gap
**EN:** "Tell me about a time you had a strong feeling about your own game — like 'I always lose the third set' or 'I play better with a certain partner' — but couldn't actually prove it."
**IT:** *"Raccontami di una volta in cui hai avuto una sensazione forte sul tuo gioco — tipo 'perdo sempre il terzo set' o 'gioco meglio con un certo compagno' — ma non riuscivi a dimostrarlo davvero."*

Probes: *What made you think that? Did you ever check if it was true? How? / Cosa ti ha fatto pensare questo? Hai mai verificato se fosse vero? Come?*

### Q2 — Current tracking behavior
**EN:** "Do you currently track or write down anything about your matches — scores, partners, how you felt? Where, and why (or why not)?"
**IT:** *"Attualmente tieni traccia o annoti qualcosa sulle tue partite — punteggi, compagni, come ti sei sentito? Dove, e perché (o perché no)?"*

Probes: *Paper, notes app, group chat, nothing? What would make you start? / Carta, app di note, chat di gruppo, niente? Cosa ti farebbe iniziare?*

### Q3 — Post-match phone ritual
**EN:** "Walk me through what you do with your phone in the 10 minutes right after a match ends."
**IT:** *"Descrivimi cosa fai con il telefono nei 10 minuti subito dopo la fine di una partita."*

Probes: *Who do you message? What do you say? Would logging a match fit into that moment, or fight with it? / A chi scrivi? Cosa dici? Registrare una partita si inserirebbe in quel momento o lo disturberebbe?*

### Q4 — Partner effects
**EN:** "Do you think some partners bring out a better game in you than others? How would you actually know?"
**IT:** *"Pensi che alcuni compagni ti facciano giocare meglio di altri? Come faresti a saperlo davvero?"*

Probes: *Can you name a specific partner and pattern? Would you want that confirmed or are you afraid of what you'd find? / Puoi indicare un compagno specifico e uno schema? Vorresti una conferma o hai timore di quello che scopriresti?*

### Q5 — Willingness & limits of effort
**EN:** "If something took 90 seconds after every match, what would have to be true for you to actually keep doing it after week 3?"
**IT:** *"Se qualcosa richiedesse 90 secondi dopo ogni partita, cosa dovrebbe essere vero perché tu continui a farlo anche dopo la terza settimana?"*

Probes: *Reminders? Seeing a payoff quickly? Social pressure from the group? / Promemoria? Vedere un beneficio velocemente? Pressione sociale dal gruppo?*

## Closing (~1 min)
"Anything about your own game you wish someone would just show you, no questions asked?" / *"C'è qualcosa sul tuo gioco che vorresti che qualcuno ti mostrasse, senza bisogno di chiedere?"*

Fill one block per participant immediately after the interview, while memory is fresh.

```

Best verbatim quote (EN, translated if needed):
"__________________________________________________________"

Original quote (IT, if applicable):
"__________________________________________________________"

One-line context (what prompted it — which question):
__________________________________________________________

Persona attribute this quote supports or challenges:
__________________________________________________________

Use on slide?  ☐ Yes  ☐ No   Notes: _____________________
```

**Guidance:** pick the single strongest, most specific quote per participant (avoid generic "yeah it's fine" answers). A good target is 2–3 quotes total feeding into the personas slide, each attributed as *"P2, club player, 3 years experience"* rather than a name — this is what turns the current unfounded Marco/Luca sketches into evidence-backed personas.
